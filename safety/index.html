SPARK

Safety Center

Built-in safety tools

  • Profile verification and trust signals
  • Content moderation for uploads and chats
  • In-app report and block controls
  • Date check-in and trusted-contact features

Meeting safely

  • Meet in public places for first dates or workout meetups.
  • Tell a friend where you are going.
  • Use your own transportation when possible.
  • Leave immediately if something feels off.

Report concerns

If someone behaves in a way that feels unsafe, abusive, fraudulent, or exploitative, use the in-app reporting tools and email safety@sparkfit.app if you need follow-up support.

Emergency help

SPARK is not an emergency service. If you are in immediate danger, contact local emergency services right away.
