How SPARK Works

Pick your mode: gym buddy, fitness community, or dating — your choice, no pressure.

01

Create Your Profile

Link HealthKit to auto-verify your workouts. Set your goals, preferred activities, and whether you're open to dating, just buddies, or both.

02

Find Your Crew

Browse workout-verified profiles by activity, schedule, and location. Choose Buddy Mode for platonic training partners or Dating Mode for romantic connections.

03

Train Together

Plan gym sessions, running dates, or hike meetups with real people who actually show up. Track shared consistency and build real connection — whatever that looks like.

Real People. Real Workouts.

Verified by HealthKit

Every profile shows genuine workout activity from Apple Health. No bots. No fake gym selfies. No influencers posting curated content — just real people who train.

Workout-Verified Profiles

Every profile includes real Apple Health workout data — duration, type, frequency, and consistency. You see what they actually do, not what they say they do.

Anti-Bot by Design

HealthKit verification makes fake profiles trivially detectable. No screenshots, no uploaded selfies — genuine Apple Health data is the signal, not curated content.

Activity Breakdown

See workout types (running, lifting, cycling, yoga), average duration, consistency streaks, and recent activity — all from HealthKit, all privacy-controlled.

Inclusive Design

Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

SPARK is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance

We aim to conform to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. This includes providing text alternatives, keyboard navigation, sufficient color contrast, and resizable text.

Accessibility Features

  • Full keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader optimized with ARIA labels
  • Skip navigation links
  • Color contrast ratios meet WCAG AA (4.5:1 minimum)
  • Reduced motion support via prefers-reduced-motion
  • Resizable text up to 200% without loss of content
  • Alternative text for all informative images
  • Semantic HTML structure

Known Limitations

Some third-party content, such as embedded social media widgets, may not be fully accessible. We are working with our partners to improve accessibility across all integrated content.

Contact Us

If you experience any accessibility issues or have feedback, please email us at hello@sparkfit.app. We take accessibility seriously and will respond within 2 business days.
