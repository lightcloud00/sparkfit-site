Inclusive Design

Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

SPARK is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance

We aim to conform to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. This includes providing text alternatives, keyboard navigation, sufficient color contrast, and resizable text.

Accessibility Features

  • Full keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader optimized with ARIA labels
  • Skip navigation links
  • Color contrast ratios meet WCAG AA (4.5:1 minimum)
  • Reduced motion support via prefers-reduced-motion
  • Resizable text up to 200% without loss of content
  • Alternative text for all informative images
  • Semantic HTML structure

Known Limitations

Some third-party content, such as embedded social media widgets, may not be fully accessible. We are working with our partners to improve accessibility across all integrated content.

Contact Us

If you experience any accessibility issues or have feedback, please email us at hello@sparkfit.app. We take accessibility seriously and will respond within 2 business days.
