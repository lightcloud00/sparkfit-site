For Developers

SPARK API

Integrate fitness matching, social features, and community tools into your own platform.

api-example.js 
const spark = await fetch('https://api.sparkfit.app/v2/match', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${API_KEY}` },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    fitness_type: 'crossfit',
    intensity: 'advanced',
    radius_miles: 10
  })
});
api_example.py 
import requests

response = requests.post(
    'https://api.sparkfit.app/v2/match',
    headers={'Authorization': f'Bearer {API_KEY}'},
    json={
        'fitness_type': 'crossfit',
        'intensity': 'advanced',
        'radius_miles': 10
    }
)
matches = response.json()
APIExample.swift 
let url = URL(string: "https://api.sparkfit.app/v2/match")!
var request = URLRequest(url: url)
request.httpMethod = "POST"
request.setValue("Bearer \(apiKey)", forHTTPHeaderField: "Authorization")

let body: [String: Any] = [
    "fitness_type": "crossfit",
    "intensity": "advanced",
    "radius_miles": 10
]

API Endpoints

POST /v2/matches Find compatible matches
GET /v2/users/{id} Get user profile
GET /v2/workouts List workouts
POST /v2/events Create community event
Rate Limit: 1,000 requests/hour
Auth: Bearer token (OAuth 2.0)
Format: JSON / REST

FAQ

Questions? Answered.

SPARK is the #1 fitness-first dating and social app. It connects people through shared fitness interests — whether you're looking for a workout partner (Buddy Mode), a romantic connection (Spark Mode), or a fitness community (Community Mode). Our AI-powered matching goes beyond photos to find people who truly fit your lifestyle.
Our AI Compatibility Engine analyzes your fitness type, workout schedule, intensity level, goals, personality traits, and lifestyle preferences. Unlike traditional dating apps, we match on the things that predict long-term compatibility — shared habits, values, and activity levels.
Yes! SPARK's core features are completely free — Buddy Mode, Community Mode, basic Spark Mode with 10 daily swipes, and workout tracking. Premium ($14.99/mo) and Elite ($24.99/mo) tiers unlock unlimited matches, advanced AI coaching, priority support, and exclusive events.
Every user starts with a base Trust Score. It increases through verified activities: completing workouts, receiving positive community feedback, passing photo verification, and maintaining consistent, respectful behavior. Trust Scores are visible on profiles so you always know who you're connecting with.
SPARK partners with six charities. When you click a donation link on our site, you're taken directly to that charity's official donation page. 100% of your donation goes straight to the charity — SPARK doesn't take a cut.
